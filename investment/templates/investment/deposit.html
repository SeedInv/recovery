To make a deposit into your investment account:

INSTRUCTIONS:

  • Click on the wallet address below to copy it to your clipboard.
  • Open your cryptocurrency wallet (e.g., MetaMask, Trust Wallet, or any other wallet you use).
  • Go to the "Send" or "Transfer" section of your wallet.
  • Paste the copied wallet address into the recipient's address field.
  • Enter the amount you wish to deposit. Make sure you're sending the correct cryptocurrency (e.g., Bitcoin, Ethereum, etc.).
  • Confirm all the details are correct, then submit the transaction.
  • Once the transaction is complete, the funds will appear in your account.

Please double-check the wallet address and the cryptocurrency you're sending to avoid any errors. If you're unsure, please contact support for assistance.
